## What Is IndieGameDrop?

IndieGameDrop tracks **83,000+ Steam games** to surface the indie games that deserve your attention. We use real data — review velocity, player counts, follower trends — to find rising gems before they blow up.

No hype. No sponsored lists. Just data.

## What We Track

- **🚀 Fastest Rising** — Games gaining momentum right now
- **💎 Hidden Gems** — Highly-rated games flying under the radar
- **🆕 New Releases** — Fresh indie drops worth watching
- **📊 Full Browse** — Search and filter the entire database
